This course is tailored for aspiring game developers, computer science students, hobbyists, indie game developers, and educators eager to delve into the exciting world of game design and development. Whether you're a novice exploring the fundamentals or an enthusiast seeking to enhance your skills, this course offers valuable insights and practical knowledge applicable to game creation across various platforms.

Participants should possess basic computer literacy and familiarity with using web browsers and software applications. An interest in learning about game design and development is essential to fully engage with the course content. With these foundational elements in place, participants can embark on a rewarding journey into the intricacies of game creation and development.

Throughout this course, learners will identify foundational concepts and principles of game design and the game development process. They'll create and deploy simple games using HTML5, focusing on game loops, graphics, and sound. Additionally, they'll explore operations within the Unreal Engine, navigating its interface and utilizing its tools for game development. By course end, participants will acquire practical skills in Unreal Engine to design, develop, and complete a basic game project, empowering them to bring their creative visions to life in the digital realm.

In the rapidly evolving realm of game development, the transition from foundational concepts to mastering advanced platforms like Unreal Engine marks a pivotal journey for beginners and intermediate enthusiasts alike. This short course is crafted to demystify this path, offering a structured and immersive learning experience that bridges the gap between basic game design principles and the complexities of modern game engines.
